# Code of Conduct

Remote Dev Skillkit follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version
2.1.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education,
socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the safety model.
- Showing empathy toward other community members.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, personal attacks, or sexualized language.
- Trolling or deliberately derailing technical discussion.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Encouraging unsafe remote-control behavior, hidden persistence, credential
  theft, or bypassing user consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, commits, code, issues, and other
contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct. Maintainers may ban
contributors temporarily or permanently for behavior they deem inappropriate,
threatening, offensive, harmful, or unsafe for this project.

## Reporting

Until a dedicated security/community email is configured, report conduct issues
privately to the repository owner.
